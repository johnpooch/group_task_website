Where to get your fix

Caffeine is your best friend during the course. There are loads of places near the Code Intitue to grab a coffee and refuel. Our team have found the best local coffee spots so you can get your fix when you need it.

Dwarf Jar

Dwarf Jar Coffee is for our traditional coders. The barista’s are always friendly and the cakes are always sweet. Dwarf Jar’s massive window offer lovely views of the river Liffey and it’s the perfect spot for watching the world go by on this busy city quay. Whether you’re in the mood for a quick expresso, a sweet crepe or one of their wide variety of teas, Dwarf Jar offers it all with a friendly smile.

The Ink Factory

The Ink Factory is not only one of Dublin’s trendiest tattoo and piercing establishments, but also a brilliant option for coffee. They are located two doors down from the Code Institute. A great choice for trendy coders!

Costa Coffee

Costa Coffee is for our 'no-frills-attached' coders. While slightly more expensive than our other suggestions, Costa offer large sizes for coders who need a jumbo coffee fix. Located directly behind the Code Institute, Costa is perfect when you need your coffee in a hurry!
